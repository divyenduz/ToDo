Be Productive!!

ToDo List based on
Urgent Important Matrix

Features

1. Add tasks as Urgent or important or both or none
2. App will sort your tasks in following order
Urgent + Important (Critical) ! Important (> Urgent) ! Urgent (Interruptive e.g. call) ! Time waste (Avoid) !
3. Try to have max tasks as green/blue
4. Only unavoidable reds
5. Minimize time waste by avoiding black
6. Stick to the plan ;)
